Hello there!

Leonardo, here.

I am a PhD candidate in the Department of Industrial Engineering - PUC-Rio, Rio de Janeiro, 🇧🇷.

My current interests are application of statistical and machine learning methods to tidy data into knowledge 📊.

I have applied statistical modeling, machine learning, and data-driven concepts within Healthcare analytics 👨‍⚕️ . Some of those experiences comprise: an overview of COVID-19 admissions in Brazil; the evaluation of efficiency and organizational factors in intensive care units; the identification of seasonal patterns in Stroke admissions as well as process optimization and mathematical modelling along with process mining techniques. I am #rstats user, but I have been playing along with #python and database concepts 💻 (Github).

I am a learning enthusiast , and I love to discuss new topics, data science , games 🎮 and coffee ☕. You can easily find me on twitter ✉ or contact me on other social accounts 😄.
